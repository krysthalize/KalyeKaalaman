The Struggles of Homeless Children in Accessing Education in the Philippines

Homeless children in the Philippines face significant barriers to education, including lack of permanent addresses, transportation issues, and inadequate parental support. These factors prevent them from enrolling in formal schooling, and while alternative learning systems (ALS) exist, they are underfunded and limited in reach. Expanding inclusive education programs and improving government and NGO efforts are crucial to providing better access to education for homeless children.

The Role of NGOs in Providing Education for Homeless Children in Metro Manila

NGOs in Metro Manila provide homeless children with educational opportunities through mobile learning programs and alternative learning systems (ALS). These organizations offer flexible, non-formal education tailored to the unique circumstances of homeless children, alongside essential support services like health care and emotional assistance. NGOs play a key role in bridging the gap between street life and formal education.

The Impact of Street Life on the Education of Homeless Children

Street life negatively impacts homeless children's education by exposing them to trauma, malnutrition, and lack of stability. Emotional and psychological distress, as well as poor nutrition, hinder their ability to learn. Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes psychological support, health services, and educational programs tailored to the needs of street children.

Policy Recommendations for Improving Education for Homeless Children in the Philippines

Current policies in the Philippines fail to adequately address the unique educational needs of homeless children. Recommendations include flexible enrollment processes, expanded alternative learning systems (ALS), better coordination between the government and NGOs, and stronger enforcement of child protection laws. These measures would help homeless children gain access to education and break the cycle of poverty.
