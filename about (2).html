Discover the Heart of KalyeKaalaman

KalyeKaalaman began as a heartfelt initiative by a group of HUMSS students who shared a strong belief in the importance of equal access to education for all. Witnessing the struggles of street children in Taguig City, they were inspired to take action, starting with a simple tutoring program. This small project quickly evolved into a full-fledged advocacy program that not only provides academic support but also equips children with life skills and health programs to nurture their minds, bodies, and spirits.

The inspiration for KalyeKaalaman runs even deeper, rooted in the personal journey of a former teacher who was once homeless. Through the kindness of a stranger, she was given the chance to transform her life. After graduating and becoming a teacher, she made it her mission to give back, helping homeless children just as she had once been helped. This teacher's story resonated deeply with the HUMSS students and played a pivotal role in shaping their vision. Her experience of overcoming adversity and becoming an agent of change served as the spark that ignited their shared dream for inclusive education.

The founders are proud of how far their efforts have come and remain committed to creating a future where every child has the chance to learn, grow, and succeed, regardless of their background. Together, they are turning this vision into reality, one child at a time.
